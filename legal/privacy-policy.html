MangaToucan Legal

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data MangaToucan processes, how that data is used, and what choices you have. The current version of MangaToucan is designed so core reading enhancement and billing checks run on-device rather than through a general MangaToucan account backend.

1. What data the app processes

MangaToucan stores a limited set of account and app data locally on your device, which may include:

  • your chosen sign-in method;
  • your email address;
  • a provider user identifier, if available;
  • a local install identifier;
  • onboarding answers and onboarding completion status;
  • membership status derived from Apple StoreKit.

If you choose Apple Sign In or Google Sign In, Apple or Google also process your information under their own privacy policies. Apple processes subscription and payment-related information for billing.

2. What we do not intend to collect

MangaToucan is not intended to send your manga pages, reading URLs, reading history, payment card details, or third-party manga-site credentials to MangaToucan servers. The app’s colorization path is on-device. Payment card and subscription billing details are handled by Apple.

3. How we use data

We use local account data to let you sign in on a device, restore your local profile, show membership status, personalize onboarding state, and operate the app. Where required by law, we rely on legal bases such as contract performance, legitimate interests, compliance with legal obligations, or your consent.

4. Sharing and disclosure

For the current no-backend version of the app, MangaToucan does not operate a general user account server that receives your local profile data in the ordinary course of app use. However, Apple, Google, and other providers you choose to use may receive data necessary to provide sign-in, billing, subscription management, or device platform services.

We may also disclose information where required by law or where reasonably necessary to protect rights, safety, security, or prevent fraud or abuse.

5. Retention

Local account and onboarding data is stored on your device until you sign out, delete account data in Settings, uninstall the app, or clear device storage. Apple and Google may retain information under their own policies. Apple purchase history and subscriptions are managed through your Apple account and are not deleted by deleting local account data in the app.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the data we control. Because the app stores profile data locally, the security of your device, Apple account, Google account, backups, and installed software also affects privacy and security. No system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

7. Children

MangaToucan is not intended for children below the minimum age permitted by law in their jurisdiction to provide personal data without the appropriate consent. If you believe a child has provided personal data inappropriately, contact MangaToucan@gmail.com.

8. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, export, restrict, or object to the processing of your personal data, and to withdraw consent where consent is the legal basis.

In the app, you can export local account data and delete local account data from Settings. You may also contact MangaToucan@gmail.com for privacy requests. If you use Apple or Google sign-in, or Apple billing, you may need to exercise some rights directly with those providers as well.

9. International transfers

If personal data is processed by Apple, Google, email providers, support tools, or other service providers outside your jurisdiction, those transfers may involve countries with different privacy laws. Where we are responsible for such transfers, we aim to rely on a lawful transfer mechanism where required.

10. Third-party services

This policy covers MangaToucan’s own processing. Third-party manga sites, Apple, Google, and other external services have their own privacy practices. Review their policies before using those services.

11. Policy changes

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app or legal requirements change. If changes are material, we will provide notice in the app or by another appropriate method.

12. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact MangaToucan@gmail.com. You may also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority or regulator.
